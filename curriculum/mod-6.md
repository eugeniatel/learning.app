## Project

Build a 100-prompt eval for something you actually care about: DeFi-specific reasoning, stablecoin mechanics, or CDP accounting correctness. Run it on 4 models (one API, one open, one small, one frontier reasoning model). Write a report on what the eval measures and what it doesn't.
